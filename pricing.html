Frequently Asked Pricing Questions

How is pricing determined for custom homes?

We calculate based on square footage, siding material complexity (stucco vs vinyl), and severity of mold or lichen growth.

Do I need to be home for the washing?

No, as long as all windows are tightly closed, water spigots are turned on, and access gates are unlocked, our team can clean safely.

Are water source costs included?

Our rigs hook up to your external residential water spigot. We can bring water on-board for properties without active spigots for a small fee.
